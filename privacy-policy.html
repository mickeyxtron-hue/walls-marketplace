Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Walls ("we", "our", "us") is a property marketplace app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number when you register.
  • Listing Information: Property details, photos, and contact information you provide when creating a listing.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (listings viewed, searches, favourites).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Location: Approximate location (if you grant permission) to show nearby listings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your property listings.
  • To connect buyers and sellers.
  • To improve our app and services.
  • To send notifications about new listings or messages (if you opt-in).

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your contact details (phone and email) are only shared with other users when you choose to list a property or respond to an inquiry.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers and on MongoDB Atlas. We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

  • You can access, update, or delete your account and listings at any time.
  • You can opt out of push notifications in your device settings.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
